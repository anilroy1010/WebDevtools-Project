Click down to know more-About WebSick

A term coined by Anil Roy on 19 November 2024 to embody the passion and curiosity for everything web-related. Just like being homesick means longing for home, being WebSick reflects an unrelenting eagerness to explore, learn, and innovate within the world of the internet. Whether you're building your first website or diving into advanced web technologies, WebSick is a community for everyone who feels at home on the internet. Are you WebSick too? Let's explore this place and grow together in this ever-evolving digital space.
